The Inventor

Peter Forcier

Peter Forcier is the inventor behind the #30 Catcher+, with over 40 years of HVAC experience under his belt. A proud native of Keene, NH, he spent his career in the heating trade helping people stay warm — and finding ways to make the job less of a grind.

Long nights on call, 40+ hour shifts during brutal New England winters, and decades of wrestling with heavy Extrol tanks — it all added up. One day, mid-job, Peter had a thought: "There must be an Easier Way."

That moment sparked the invention and patent of the EasyWay #30 Catcher Plus. Having owned his own business, Peter knows the pressures professionals face — and he built this tool specifically to take one of those pressures off the table.

The Product

What Is the #30 Catcher+?

After many years in the industry, Peter wanted to make managing #30 and #60 Extrol tanks safer and simpler. When completely filled, these tanks can weigh up to 60 lbs — making them difficult to handle and prone to creating hazardous, costly messes.

The #30 Catcher+ is a heavy-duty catching bag made from rip-resistant polyester fiber and industrial-grade mesh, with reinforced steel grappling hooks that lock securely onto the tank. Multi-use and built to last — your whole team will be saying "That was Easy."
